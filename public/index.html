Pure Cleaner

Pure Cleaner is an all-in-one utility app to help you clean and organize your phone. Our features include cleaning duplicate and similar photos, compressing large videos, and managing your contacts.

Gmail Cleaning Feature

A key feature of Pure Cleaner is the ability to clean your email inbox. Our app securely connects to your Gmail account to find and delete old newsletters, spam, and promotional emails you no longer want.

How it Works: Pure Cleaner requests your permission to access your Gmail account. This allows our app to scan your emails and show you a list of items you can safely delete. We only use this permission to find and remove emails as you direct. We do not store your emails, and we do not share your data with anyone.

Pure Cleaner's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.
