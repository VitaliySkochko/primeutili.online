1. Categories of Personal Data We Collect

  • Data you provide: gender, age category, email, feedback, and other info you voluntarily enter.
  • Data collected automatically: IP address, device type and model, OS version, time zone, language settings, hardware ID, advertising ID (IDFA/GAID), usage patterns, cookies.
  • Data from third parties: analytics and marketing partners may provide data on how you found our app or website.

2. Purposes of Processing

We process your personal data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service.
  • Analyze usage and improve performance (using Google Analytics, Firebase, Amplitude, Appsflyer, Facebook, Snapchat).
  • Serve and measure ads, including personalized advertising.
  • Communicate with you (support, notifications, offers).
  • Process payments for premium features or subscriptions.
  • Enforce our Terms and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Bases (EEA Users)

For users in the EEA, we process personal data under the following bases:

  • Contract performance (providing the Service).
  • Legitimate interests (analyzing usage, sending marketing messages, improving features).
  • Legal obligations (tax, law enforcement).

4. Sharing of Personal Data

We share your data with trusted third-party providers:

  • Cloud hosting (Google Firebase).
  • Analytics (Google, Appsflyer, Amplitude, Facebook, Snapchat).
  • Marketing partners (ad networks, email delivery).
  • Payment processors.
  • Law enforcement or regulators, when legally required.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and correct your data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Restrict or object to processing.

6. Age Limitation

We do not knowingly collect data from persons under 16. If you learn that a child has provided data, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred and stored outside your country of residence. Where applicable, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and other safeguards.

8. Google API Services User Data Policy

Pure Cleaner's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

To provide our email cleaning service, our app requests access to your Google user data. We use this access only to read email metadata (like sender and date) to identify unwanted mail and to move emails to the trash when you instruct the app to do so.

  • We do not store your email content.
  • We do not share, sell, or transfer your Google user data to any third party (except as required for legal compliance).
  • The scopes we request are limited to the minimum required for the app's functionality.

14. Data Security and Protection

We take the protection of your data, especially sensitive Google user data, very seriously. We implement the following data protection mechanisms to keep your information safe:

  • Encryption in Transit: All communication between the Pure Cleaner app and Google's servers is encrypted using industry-standard Transport Layer Security (TLS/SSL).
  • Data at Rest: We do not permanently store your email content, email bodies, or attachments on our servers. When you use the email cleaning feature, metadata (like sender and date) is processed in real-time and is not stored after your session is complete.
  • Limited Use: We strictly adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy's Limited Use requirements. Your Gmail data is only used to provide the email cleaning feature as described. It is never used for advertising, sold to third parties, or analyzed by humans, except as required for security purposes or legal compliance.
  • Access Control: We use secure OAuth 2.0 authentication tokens to access your data. You can revoke this access at any time via your Google Account security settings. Access to any user data on our backend systems is strictly limited to authorized personnel for the sole purpose of maintaining and securing the service.
